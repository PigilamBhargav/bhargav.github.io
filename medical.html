Courses under NEET:

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is the single entrance test conducted for undergraduate medical admission in the country. The list of courses under NEET 2023 is long and offers a promising career to candidates. Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) and Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) are the two most sought-after courses under the NEET courses list.
Additionally, candidates are granted admission to several other courses after qualifying for NEET. By cracking the NEET exam, candidates can pursue different courses in the medical field after class 12. Authorities grant admission to MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BYNS, BUMS, BSMS, BHMS, BVSc & AH, nursing courses through NEET scores. Eligible students are shortlisted for seats in a respective medical course after class 12 on the basis of NEET results.

NOTE:

To pursue courses under NEET, candidates must have qualified class 12 with a minimum of 50% marks (UR category) and 40% (reserved category) marks. Candidates must have studied Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as compulsory subjects.

1. MBBS

  • Course fee range:The MBBS course fee ranges from as low as 1,355 to as high as 25,75,000.
  • Scope of range :Candidates can pursue a career as a Doctor and Physician.
  • Higher studies :Candidates can pursue higher education with a specialisation in medicine. MS, MD, and DNB are the popular postgraduate medical degrees.

2. BDS

  • Course fee range:The average BDS course fee ranges between Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 12,00,000.
  • Scope of range :Candidates can pursue a career as a Dentist, Dental Technician, Prosthodontist, Dental Hygienist, and Dental Consultant.
  • Higher studies :Candidates can pursue Master's in Dental Surgery in a choice of specialisation.

3. BAMS

  • Course fee range:The annual fee for the BAMS course on average ranges between Rs. 25,000 to Rs. 3,20,000
  • Scope of range : Pharmacist, Dietician, Gynaecologist, Teacher, Panchkarma Practitioner
  • Higher studies :Candidates can pursue an M.S. (Master of Surgery) (Ayurveda) and an M.D. (Doctor of Medicine) (Ayurveda) in specialisation.

4. BHMS

  • Course fee range:The range of BHMS course fee is between Rs. 20,000 to Rs. 300,000.
  • Scope of range :Homeopathic Doctor, Public Health Specialist, Pharmacist, Teacher or Lecturer
  • Higher studies :Candidates can pursue MD / MS / PG Diploma in Homeopathy.

5. BNYS

  • Course fee range:The course fee ranges from Rs 38, 200 to 5, 96,500.
  • Scope of range :Naturopathy physician, therapist, heath supervisor, consultant, acupuncture, neuro-physiology, speciality clinics
  • Specialisations Offerd :Nutrition Therapy, Natural Childbirth, Herbal/Botanical Medicine, Homeopathic Medicine, and Acupuncture.

6. BUMS

  • Course fee range:The range of BUMS fee is from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 6,50,000.
  • Scope of range :Scientist, Consultant, Pharmacist, Therapist, Drug Inspector, Medical Assistant, Professor
  • Higher studies :Candidates can study MD / MS / PG Diploma courses in Unani medicine.

7. BVSc & AH

  • Course fee range:The fee for BVSc & AH courses ranges between Rs. 10,000 to Rs. 1,00,000.
  • Scope of range :Veterinary surgeon, Pharmacologist, Food Inspection Specialist, and Veterinarian Assistant.
  • Higher studies :Students can pursue a Masters in Veterinary Science (M.V.Sc) in a specialisation of their choice. Specialisations include Veterinary Bacteriology, Epidemiology, Veterinary Immunology, and Livestock Economics to name a few.

8. BPT

  • Course fee range:The fee ranges between Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 7,10,000.
  • Scope of range :Physiotherapist at health clinics, MNCs, industries, and private practice.
  • Higher studies :After BPT, students can pursue a Master of Physiotherapy (MPT) which is a two-year-long course.

9. BSc. nursing

  • Course fee range:The fee ranges between Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 3,50,000.
  • Scope of range :Staff Nurse, Registered Nurse (RN), Nurse Educator, Medical Coder, Registered Nurse (RN) - Emergency Room, Nurse - Intensive Care Unit (ICU)
  • Higher studies :Candidates who want to go for further studies can join for MSc Nursing, MSc Neuroscience, MSc Medical Microbiology, MSc Biotechnology, and MSc Biochemistry. Moreover, B.Sc Nursing graduates can also join a postgraduate program in Public Health management besides MSc in renal sciences and dialysis technology.
